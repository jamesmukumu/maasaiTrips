What is Included in a Masaai Mara Safari

Game Drives

Experience thrilling morning and evening game drives with expert guides who navigate the vast savannah, bringing you close to the Big Five and other wildlife. directions_car

Accommodations

Stay in carefully selected lodges, tented camps, or luxury resorts that provide comfort, excellent service, and often stunning views of the Mara plains. apartment

Meals

Our packages include full-board meals, allowing you to enjoy local and international cuisine amidst the African wilderness. restaurant_menu

Cultural Experiences

The safaris include visits to Maasai villages, where you can learn about the rich traditions and daily life of the Maasai people. groups

Airport Transfers

Fly~in or "Flyng Safaris" as these are also known offer the convenience of getting to Masai Mara within an hour from Nairobi and are norm for certain Safari Camps in Maasai Mara which are more remote or those that only offer packaged stays including safari drives. Flights take off from Wilson airport in Nairobi twice daily and upon arrival at the airstrip in the Mara, guests are received and transfered to their respective Camp or Lodge in time for lunch.
